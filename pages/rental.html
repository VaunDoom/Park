Pavilion rental at Community Park

A required $100.00 deposit will hold the date you want. It is advisable to pay this as soon as feasible to assure the date you are wanting. A separate rental fee is due for the rental of the pavilion of $100.00 per day and is due at least ten (10) days prior to the event. Please call Fran for more information at 419-884-7259 or email.

Cabin Rental

Rent the Cabin in Community Park. A required $100.00 deposit is necessary to hold the date and should be put down as soon as feasible. The deposit is returned upon inspection of the cabin after your event. The rental fee of $100.00 for the day is required at least ten (10) days prior to your event. Please call Fran for rental information at 419-884-7259 or Email
